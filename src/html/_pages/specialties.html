Get inside the motivations and relationships behind the decisions.

What’s the trick to b2b communications? Gloss over the price, romance the features, call it a solution and call it a day. Right? Well, no. Most decision makers spend about half of their waking hours thinking about their business. Their professional goals are interwoven with their business goals.

What do they want to achieve? How can they get there? How can what you have to offer help them? Know this and you know them. Antfarm has spent much of the past 20 years helping companies develop insights and strategies for their b2b customers. We’ve helped companies sell tools to tradesmen, building products to contractors, valves to engineers, broadband to business owners, servers to IT pros, and more.

Focus for tactics, inspiration for creativity.

There’s one customer that’s usually taken for granted in every marketing campaign: your team. Each person on your team has their own assumptions, inclinations and motivations. And sometimes they’re your toughest customer. That’s why the first “ad” of every campaign should be to your own team. Give them a clear direction to be successful, inspire them with fresh insights, and you stand a chance of selling your real customers.

The Antfarm approach to strategy is specifically designed to help your team work efficiently and effectively get to new and better ideas. We’ve developed communication strategies to help companies sell washing machines and car washes, fast food and haute cuisine, plant automation and server virtualization. And many, many more.

Technology

Something so innovative should be oh so easy to sell. Surprisingly, it’s the newest technology that’s the hardest to communicate to your customers. How will this make their life better? What’s wrong with what they already have? Sometimes they can’t hear your pitch over all the bells and whistles?

But as fast as technology changes, people are constant. The want faster, cheaper, more control, more connection, they want to do more with less. The best stories about technology are the stories of the people technology serves.

Antfarm has helped uncover the human story for a variety of technology products, both consumer and industrial. We’ve worked on smart distributed valves, virtualized servers and ultra-rugged barcode scanners as well as laptops, wireless handsets, GPS devices and a social media application for classic car aficionados.
