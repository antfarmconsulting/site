Customer Video, Personae, Interviews, Cultural Analysis

Video and personae are powerful tools for communicating information and insights, helping decision makers better understand the customer they hope to influence. Antfarm has created Customer Videos and Personae of corporate IT leaders, jeans shoppers, football fans, new parents, recent hospital patients, and many others.

Allowing customers to tell their story in their own words is often provides unexpected insights into their situation. Antfarm has conducted Interviews at car rallies, street corners, industry conferences, restaurants, tail-gaiters, c-suites, and of course focus group facilities.

Insights are all about the right perspective, and sometimes that perspective is the culture in which a business operates. Antfarm has conducted Cultural Analyses of restaurant scenes from movies for a quick service restaurant, published player biographies for an NFL franchise, and advertising images of female beauty for a cosmetics marketer.

Concept Testing, Competitive Analysis, Evaluation, Retail Experience

Have an idea for a product, package, or communication? Antfarm helps its clients understand customer reactions to new ideas and helps make them stronger. We've conducted Concept Testing for a new restaurant, packaging for construction contractors, ideas for frozen meals and household cleaners, websites, direct mail, and many, many ads.

When your product is your store, there are endless opportunities to delight, distract, and confuse your customers. We've brought together our clients and customers for Retail Experience workshops to improve hotel service and car washes, we've watched contractors interacting with promotional material at big box stores, and interviewed shoppers as they navigate the frozen food section of a grocery chain.
